Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe

Here is a simple recipe that will make a hot buffalo chicken dip for game day!

Ingredients

  • 1 package of cream cheese (8 ounce package)
  • 2 cups of shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo wing sauce
  • 1/2 cup ranch or blue cheese salad dressing
  • 2 cups shredded Colby-Monterey Jack cheese

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350ยบ F
  • Mix together all ingredients except last 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese into a medium baking dish.
  • Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup of shredded cheddar cheese on top of dip
  • Bake for 15-20 minutes or until hot and bubbly
  • Remove from the oven
  • Serve with celery sticks, fritos, pita chips, or tortilla chips.
